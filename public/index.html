Before you buy

Prices can change, sales can end, and plans can vary by country. Always check the host’s checkout page before buying.

  • Check monthly renewal price, not only the first-month discount.
  • Pick a server location close to your players.
  • For modpacks, CPU quality matters as much as RAM.
  • Check backups, file access, support speed, and refund policy.
