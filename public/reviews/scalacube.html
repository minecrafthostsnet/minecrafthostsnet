Cons

Who should choose ScalaCube?

  • Testing a server idea
  • Small friend groups
  • Beginners who want one-click tools

ScalaCube makes the most sense when your priority is cheap starter servers. If your server is public, heavily modded, or has many players online at once, check CPU and RAM limits carefully.
