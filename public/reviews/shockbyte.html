Who should choose Shockbyte?

  • First Minecraft servers
  • Small public communities
  • Owners who want simple setup

Shockbyte makes the most sense when your priority is beginner-friendly minecraft hosting. If your server is public, heavily modded, or has many players online at once, check CPU and RAM limits carefully.
